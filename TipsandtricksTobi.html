Couple tips to get off the bat

  1. Can NOT be walking on the grass buddy, it's to pay respect to the buried slaves
    because the school was built on the former Alta Vista Plantation.

  2. That deadline is going to come faster than you think, probably best to get that homework done first
  3. Once you have your class schedule, get your parking pass ASAP, they go fast trust me.
    You don't want to have an 8am at SR Collins and gotta park at Hobart Texas, not the life you want to live :/
  4. Your class says mandatory textbook, please do not buy it if the homework isn't graded in the book, check out Library Genesis they probably have the pdf.
  5. Panther bucks, there are a couple places close to campus that accept them and some that dont:
    • Obviously everywhere on Campus i.e Chick fil a, Dunkin yaddi yaddi yada
    • Dominoes
    • Checkers
    • CVS
    • We'll leave a link in the description
  6. Personally I do not use the shuttle but seems like its pretty efficient and you've already paid for it so hey might as well
    utilize the amenities provided.
  7. There are so many things happening on campus, cmon school spirit and that.
